How To Play

Build a stable funnel system that matches the selected target distribution.

  • Drag on blank space to draw a lever.
  • Drag lever body to move, drag lever ends to rotate.
  • Long-press or right-click to add/delete pegs and levers.
  • Score uses only the last 100 balls, so consistency matters.
  • Use Free Play to experiment with no target.
